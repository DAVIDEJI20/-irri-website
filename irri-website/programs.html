What We Do

Our Programs

From medical care to a first paycheque, our programs meet people where they are and walk with them to where they want to be.

Program Areas

Five Pillars of Inclusive Recovery

IRRI's work is organized around five integrated program areas, each addressing a critical dimension of the rehabilitation and reintegration journey. Every program is led by persons with disabilities and grounded in rights-based, evidence-driven, and community-centered approaches.

Integrated Program Areas

Program 01

Medical Rehabilitation & Assistive Access

When someone loses mobility or function through conflict or disaster, the first step is restoring what can be restored. We connect people to clinical rehabilitation, physiotherapy, prosthetics, and assistive devices, while providing the mental health support that makes physical recovery possible. Every plan is built around the individual, not the diagnosis.

  • Clinical rehabilitation and physiotherapy
  • Prosthetics, orthotics, and assistive devices
  • Mental health and psychosocial support (MHPSS)
  • Individualized recovery planning
Learn More
Program 02

Rehab-to-Work Pathways

Recovery does not end at the clinic door. Our Rehab-to-Work pipeline turns rehabilitation outcomes into real livelihoods. Through market-aligned vocational training, digital skills, apprenticeships, and micro-enterprise support, people gain the tools to earn a living and participate in their local economies.

  • Phone repair, IT, tailoring, and agribusiness tracks
  • Job placement and employer referrals
  • Starter toolkits and micro-grants
  • Mentorship and peer networks
Learn More
Program 03

Legal Protection & Benefits Navigation

Rights are only as strong as people's ability to claim them. We help individuals navigate pensions, entitlements, legal documentation, and compensation claims so their protections are not just written down, but acted on. Where systemic barriers exist, we advocate for change alongside the people affected.

  • Pension and entitlement claims assistance
  • Legal documentation and representation
  • Benefits navigation clinics
  • Systemic advocacy and policy engagement
Learn More
Program 04

Community Reintegration & Peacebuilding

Disability and conflict are deeply connected, yet persons with disabilities are routinely excluded from peace processes. IRRI creates space for inclusive dialogue, supports community reconciliation, and trains local peacebuilders to centre disability perspectives.

  • Peer support groups and networks
  • Community dialogue and stigma reduction
  • Inclusive peacebuilder training
  • Social reintegration support
Learn More
Program 05

Disability Inclusion in Humanitarian Response

In emergencies, persons with disabilities are among the most likely to be left behind. We work to make disability inclusion the standard across humanitarian response, from shelter and food distribution to health services and protection. We deploy rapid assessments, provide emergency assistive devices, and train responders on inclusive practices.

  • Rapid disability assessments in emergencies
  • Emergency assistive devices deployment
  • Inclusive responder training
  • Disability mainstreaming in partner programs
Learn More
Pilot Project

3-Month Proof of Concept

A structured pilot demonstrating the impact of integrated programming across three carefully sequenced phases.

01

Assessment & Enrollment

Community outreach, disability screening, and needs assessment across pilot sites. This phase maps existing services and builds referral pathways.

02

Rehabilitation & Skills Training

Participants receive individualised rehabilitation plans alongside market-aligned vocational training, so recovery and readiness move forward together.

03

Reintegration & Follow-up

Livelihood placement, legal documentation, peer support groups, and structured follow-up ensure progress made during the pilot translates into lasting change.
